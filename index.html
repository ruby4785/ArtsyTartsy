Our Story

Baked with Love, Designed with Passion

ArtsyTartsy is a one-woman home bakery. Every cake you see was designed and made by hand by a single baker who treats each order as her own. There is no factory and no template. What drives the work is simple: a love of baking and an eye for design.

Each cake is baked from scratch and finished with real care. The focus is on design, so every order is shaped around your idea and built to look like nothing else. For her, a cake is something to create, not just bake.
